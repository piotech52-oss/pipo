❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I track my order?

expand_more
You can track your order by going to "My Orders" in your dashboard. Click on the order number to see real-time tracking information. You'll also receive email updates when your order status changes.

What is your return policy?

expand_more
We offer a 30-day return policy on all unused items in original packaging. To initiate a return, go to "My Orders", select the order, and click "Return Item". Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

How long does shipping take?

expand_more
Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days. Express shipping takes 1-3 business days. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

How do I change my password?

expand_more
Go to "Profile Settings" in your dashboard, then click on "Change Password". Enter your current password and your new password to update.

Do you offer international shipping?

expand_more
Yes, we ship to select international locations. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Check our shipping policy for more details.
